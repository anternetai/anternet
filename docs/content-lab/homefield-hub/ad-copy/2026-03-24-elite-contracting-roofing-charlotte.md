## FINAL VERSIONS

### Variant 1 — Storm Damage Angle

**Primary Text:** March storms cracked more shingles across Charlotte than most homeowners realize. If your roof is 10+ years old, you could have damage that's invisible from the ground — and your insurance might pay for the entire replacement. Elite Contracting Group is running free inspections for Charlotte-area homes this month. Takes 20 minutes.

**CTA Button:** Get Your Free Inspection
